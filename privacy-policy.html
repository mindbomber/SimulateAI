Introduction

SimulateAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform.

This policy is designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, educational role
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials via Google OAuth
  • Profile Data: Learning preferences, progress tracking

Educational Data

  • Learning Progress: Simulation completions, scores, learning paths
  • Research Data: Ethical decision patterns (anonymized, with explicit consent)
  • Interaction Data: Platform usage patterns, feature engagement

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Page views, click patterns, session duration
  • Performance Data: Load times, error logs, feature usage

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and understand how our platform is used. You have full control over these cookies through our cookie consent system.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Required
Essential Cookies Basic platform functionality, authentication, security Session / 30 days Yes
Analytics Cookies Google Analytics, platform usage understanding 2 years No (Opt-in)
Research Cookies Educational research data collection (anonymized) 2 years No (Explicit consent)

Cookie Management

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Using our cookie consent banner when it appears
  • Accessing cookie settings in your account preferences
  • Contacting us directly to update your preferences

Educational Purposes

  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Track learning progress and achievements
  • Recommend appropriate content and simulations
  • Generate learning analytics and insights

Research Purposes (With Consent)

  • Study ethical decision-making patterns

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and data storage

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

Research Collaboration

Anonymized, aggregated data may be shared with educational research partners, but only with explicit consent and proper anonymization.

Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing for legitimate interests
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Delete personal information
  • Opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising rights

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates
  • Secure Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade hosting and security
  • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary information

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active + 3 years
  • Learning Progress: Retained for educational continuity
  • Research Data: Anonymized and retained for research purposes (with consent)
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Google Analytics settings (26 months default)
  • Cookie Consent: 13 months, then re-consent required

You can request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

Children's Privacy

SimulateAI is designed for educational use and may be used by students under 18 in supervised educational settings. We comply with COPPA and FERPA requirements:

  • Institutional consent required for users under 13
  • Limited data collection for educational purposes only
  • No behavioral advertising to children
  • Secure handling of educational records

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Additional security measures as required

About AI Ethics Simulations

Our mission is to make AI ethics education accessible and engaging for everyone

Our Mission

To democratize AI ethics education through interactive learning experiences that prepare learners for responsible AI development and deployment.

Open Source

All our simulations and educational content are open source, enabling educators worldwide to adapt and improve our materials.

Research-Based

Our content is grounded in the latest research in AI ethics, cognitive science, and educational technology.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This platform:

  • Stores learning progress locally in your browser
  • Uses anonymized analytics to improve our educational content
  • Does not share personal information with third parties
  • Complies with educational privacy standards (FERPA, COPPA)

Accessibility Statement

This platform is designed to be accessible to all learners. Features include:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast and large text options
  • Reduced motion settings for users with vestibular disorders
  • WCAG 2.1 AA compliance

If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us.
